ToniMade Logo Concepts

Premium, minimal, Apple-inspired typography.

Concept 1: Core Contrast

Heavy visual weight on "Toni", light and gray tone on "Made". Creates a distinct balance recognizable in modern tech branding.

Concept 1 (Dark Mode)

Pure white for the name, soft titanium gray for the modifier.

Concept 2: Seamless Unified

No color distinction. Extremely tight kerning ("tracking") and uniform semi-bold weight. Simple, authoritative, and clean.

Concept 3: Wide Editorial

Uppercase with wide tracking. Emits a luxury/"Pro" product feeling, similar to Apple's higher-end product sub-branding.

Concept 4: App / Favicon Monogram

A "squircle" containing precise, tightly kerned initials. Matches iOS icon aesthetic natively.
